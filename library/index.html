    Why this library exists

    Clarity before visibility.

    Most advice about therapist marketing begins with algorithms, rankings or getting more clicks.

    I believe that is the wrong place to start.

    Long before someone compares qualifications, fees or availability, they are deciding whether a therapist feels understandable, thoughtful and trustworthy.

    Every guide in this library explores how therapists can communicate more clearly without becoming louder.
