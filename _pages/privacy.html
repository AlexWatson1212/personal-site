Privacy

Privacy Policy

I only collect the information needed to reply to your enquiry or provide the updates you have requested. I will never sell your data or use it in a way that feels inconsistent with the trust this website is built upon.

Your privacy matters

This website has been created to help therapists explore ideas around professional identity, communication and private practice. Your privacy is important, and I aim to collect as little personal information as possible.

Information you choose to share

If you complete the contact form or subscribe to Practice Notes, you may provide information such as your name and email address.

I only use this information to:

  • Reply to your enquiry.
  • Send Practice Notes if you have subscribed.
  • Provide information you have specifically requested.

Your information is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing.

Analytics

Anonymous website analytics may be collected to help understand how people use the site and identify opportunities for improvement. These statistics do not personally identify you.

Cookies

This website may use essential cookies and anonymous analytics cookies to improve performance and understand general usage.

External websites

Some pages link to external websites such as YouTube, Counselling Directory or social media platforms. Those websites have their own privacy policies, which are outside my control.

How long information is kept

Enquiries are kept only for as long as reasonably necessary to respond, maintain ongoing communication or comply with legal obligations.

Your rights

You may request a copy of any personal information I hold about you, ask for it to be corrected or request its deletion, where legally applicable.
