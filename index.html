The Practice Clarity Blueprint.

The foundation of every project is the Practice Clarity Blueprint — a clear, carefully written document that captures who you are, who you help, how you work, and how your practice should be understood.

It gives you wording and direction you can use beyond your website too — in your directory profile, introductory emails, referral conversations, social media themes and future communication.

02

Every recommendation has a reason.

Nothing appears in your website, wording or visual direction unless it can be traced back to something real from the clarity process.

Evidence

What you said, repeated, demonstrated or clearly cared about during the Practice Clarity Session.

Interpretation

The patterns, themes and meaning drawn from that evidence.

Implication

What that means for your message, tone, visual direction, content and website journey.

Clear decision

The final wording, page structure, colour direction, content theme or call to action that follows from the strategy.

03

Six questions before anything else.

These are the questions that stop your website, directory profile and social media becoming vague, inconsistent or difficult to recognise yourself in.

01

Who are you as a practitioner?

Your story, values, working style, philosophy and professional identity.

02

Who do you help?

The people your communication is speaking to and the state they may be in when they arrive.

03

What change do you support?

The emotional and practical movement your work can help clients make.

04

Why might someone choose you?

Your strengths, perspective, fit, boundaries and genuine positioning.

05

How should people experience you?

The tone, atmosphere, trust signals and emotional quality of your communication.

06

What should someone do next?

The website journey, calls to action, enquiry experience and content priorities.

10

Questions people often ask.

Do I need an existing website?

No. Some people come with an existing website that no longer reflects their work. Others are starting from scratch.

Can I start with the Practice Clarity Blueprint only?

Yes. The Blueprint can stand alone. You can use it to clarify your direction, improve your current website, rewrite your directory profile, plan your content or decide whether a website is the right next step.

Do I have to buy every stage?

No. Each stage is optional. You can start with Practice Clarity and stop there, or add Visual Identity, Website design or Hosting & Care when it makes sense.

What happens in the Practice Clarity Session?

We talk through your story, values, clients, working style, positioning and communication needs. The session draws out the material your Blueprint, visual identity, website or future content can be built from.

How is the Blueprint evidence-based?

The Blueprint is based on what you say, repeat, emphasise and demonstrate during the clarity process. The aim is not to invent a brand for you, but to identify what is already there and translate it into clear communication.

Do you use AI during the process?

I may use carefully supervised tools to help organise notes, transcripts and early draft material. The judgement, interpretation, recommendations and final direction are reviewed and shaped by me.

What if I don't know what I want to say?

That is one of the most common reasons people get in touch. You do not need to arrive with perfect wording. The process is designed to help us find it.

How long does a project usually take?

It depends which stage you choose. A Practice Clarity Blueprint is quicker than a full website. Complete website projects usually take around four to six weeks.

Do you provide hosting?

Yes. I offer an optional Hosting & Care plan for hosting, maintenance, updates and support after launch.

Who do you work with?

I mainly work with newly qualified and growing therapists, counsellors, coaches and helping professionals whose work depends on trust, relationship and careful communication.
