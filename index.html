The real problem

The website is rarely the starting point.

Most people do not struggle because they need more pages, more effects or a more complicated design. They struggle because they are not yet clear on what their website needs to communicate.

Before the website comes clarity: who you are, what your work means, who you help, what makes you different and what someone needs to feel before they trust you enough to get in touch.

The website becomes the visual expression of that work. It is not the whole product. It is one outcome of a deeper positioning process.

About

Understanding people first. Building websites second.

My background is in marketing, branding and communication. I have a degree in Marketing Psychology and over ten years of experience helping businesses think about how they are seen, understood and trusted.

I originally learned website design as a practical way to bring branding, positioning and communication together. The website became the place where the thinking could be seen, felt and used.

I am also training as a counsellor, which has deepened my interest in trust, human behaviour and meaningful communication. That combination shapes the way I work: calm design, clear messaging and websites that feel honest, grounded and human.

The real product is clarity. The website is the outcome: a visible expression of the positioning, language and trust-building work that comes before it.

FAQ

Practical questions.

A few simple answers before getting in touch.

Is the Blueprint separate from the website?

Yes. The Blueprint can be used on its own, or it can become the foundation for a website.

Who is this for?

Therapists, counsellors, coaches, helping professionals and trust-based businesses.

Do I need to know what I want already?

No. The point of the process is to help you move from uncertainty to clarity.

Can you help with words and structure?

Yes. Messaging, tone, structure and calls to action are part of the process.

Will I own the website?

Yes. The website is created for you, and the aim is not to trap you in a system you do not control.
