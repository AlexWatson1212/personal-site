The problem

A website cannot fix an unclear message.

If people cannot quickly understand who you help, how you work, and why you feel safe to contact, the design can look beautiful and still fail to build trust.

Questions people often ask.

Do I need an existing website?

No. Some people come with an existing website, while others are starting from scratch.

Can I start with the Blueprint only?

Yes. The clarity work can be valuable on its own before investing in a full website.

What if I do not know what I want to say?

That is usually the reason people begin this process. The work is designed to uncover that clarity.

Can you help with the wording?

Yes. Messaging and communication are central to the process.

Is this only for therapists?

No. It is especially suited to therapists, counsellors, coaches and helping professionals whose work depends on trust.
