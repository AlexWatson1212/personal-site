About

Branding, websites and strategy for work that depends on trust.

My background is in marketing, branding and communication. I have a degree in Marketing Psychology and over ten years of experience working across different industries, helping businesses think about how they are seen, understood and trusted.

Over the years, I have worked with and built websites for a wide range of businesses, including safety and engineering companies, beauty and makeup brands, skate shops, action camera retailers, wine bars, cleaning companies, curtain fitters, massage businesses and local service providers.

I originally learned website design because I needed a practical way to bring branding, positioning and communication together. The website became the place where the strategy could be seen, felt and used.

I am now also training as a counsellor, which has deepened my interest in trust, human behaviour and meaningful communication. That combination shapes the way I work: calm design, clear messaging and websites that feel honest, grounded and human.

My aim is to help therapists, counsellors, coaches and helping professionals create thoughtful, professional websites without needing to pay agency-level fees or figure everything out alone.

Calm design. Clear message. Human trust. I bring together marketing psychology, brand thinking, website design and counselling training to help your online presence feel clear, trustworthy and true to your work.

Ownership & Practical Questions

Clear, simple and transparent.

I want the process to feel straightforward, especially if websites, hosting and domains usually feel confusing.

Will I own my website?

Yes. The website is created for you. If you decide to move away in the future, the files can be supplied so you are not trapped.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes. I can help connect your existing domain or guide you through buying one if you do not already have one.

Do I need to know what I want first?

No. Part of the work is helping you clarify your message, structure and direction before the website is built.
