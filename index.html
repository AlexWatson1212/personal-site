The philosophy

People rarely struggle because they need a better logo.

They struggle because they have not clearly defined who they are, who they help, what makes them different, why people should trust them and how they want to be remembered.

The website is not the starting point. It is the final expression of deeper decisions. Once the clarity is there, the colours, words, structure and design become much easier.

The core product

The Personal Brand Blueprint.

This is the foundation of the whole process. The website, visual identity and brand guidelines all stem from the Blueprint.

01

Identity

Story, purpose, values, personality and the kind of practitioner the client is becoming.

02

Audience

Who they help, what those people are struggling with and what they are hoping to move towards.

03

Positioning

What makes them memorable, what they should be known for and how to communicate it simply.

04

Voice

Writing style, tone, useful language, words to avoid and how the brand should sound.

05

Trust

Credentials, experience, memberships, testimonials, approach and signals of safety.

06

Visual Direction

Mood, colours, photography, typography, layout and the emotional experience of the website.

The real value

Don’t sell a website. Sell clarity.

Clients are not just paying for pages, colours or a PDF. They are paying for better positioning, greater confidence, consistency, professional presentation and a clear plan.

The website, brand guidelines and visual identity are evidence of the thinking. The deeper product is helping someone understand how to communicate who they are with confidence.

Your role

Creative director, not production machine.

The high-value work is discovery, listening, pattern recognition, positioning, website strategy and quality control.

Logo design, brand guideline layout, graphic polish and eventually parts of website development can be delegated to trusted specialists. Your role is to hold the clarity, direction and final judgement.

Validation roadmap

Build the system by proving it works.

Phase 1

Use yourself as the first case study and make every decision visible.

Phase 2

Phase 3

Phase 4

Gather feedback on clarity, confidence, usefulness and perceived value.

Phase 5

Secure the first paying client

Real client feedback will teach more than another month of planning.

About

Understanding people first. Building websites second.

I sit between marketing, branding, website design and counselling.

Frequently asked questions

A few common questions.

Do I need an existing website?

No. Some people come with an existing website, while others are starting completely from scratch.

Can I start with the Blueprint only?

Yes. The clarity work can be valuable on its own before you invest in a full website.

What if I don't know what I want to say?

That is usually the reason people contact me. The process is designed to uncover that clarity.

Can you help with wording?

Yes. Messaging and communication are central to the process, not an afterthought.

Do you only work with therapists?

Therapists are the primary focus, but I also work with coaches, wellbeing practitioners and helping professionals.
