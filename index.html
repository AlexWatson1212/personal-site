Why therapist websites are different

Your website should help people feel safe enough to contact you.

People often arrive at a therapist or helping professional's website feeling anxious, uncertain or overwhelmed.

They are not looking for clever design. They are looking for reassurance, clarity and a sense that you might be someone they can trust.

That is why I focus on calm design, clear structure and human copy that helps visitors understand your work and feel able to take the next step.

About

Websites for people whose work depends on trust.

I create calm, professional websites for therapists, counsellors, coaches and helping professionals whose work is personal, sensitive or deeply human.

The goal is not just to make your site look good. It is to help potential clients quickly understand what you offer, feel reassured by what they see and know how to contact you.

As a trainee counsellor, I understand how important it is for your website to feel ethical, grounded and trustworthy — not pushy, generic or overly salesy.

A website that helps the right people take the next step. Calm, professional design that builds trust and makes contacting you feel easy.
