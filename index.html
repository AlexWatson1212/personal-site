You became a therapist to help people, not to market yourself.

The belief

01

The Brand Clarity Blueprint.

The foundation of every project is the Brand Clarity Blueprint — a clear, carefully written document that captures who you are, who you help, how you work, and how your practice should be understood across your whole online presence.

It is not a questionnaire, a moodboard or a collection of notes. It is a carefully interpreted reflection of your professional identity, grounded in what you have actually said, shown and demonstrated.

It gives you positioning you can use beyond your website too — in your directory profile, introductory emails, referral conversations, social media themes and future communication.

02

Every recommendation has a reason.

Nothing appears in your brand, website or messaging unless it can be traced back to something real you said.

Evidence

What you said, repeated, demonstrated or clearly cared about during the Brand Clarity Session.

Interpretation

The patterns, themes and strategic meaning drawn from that evidence.

Implication

What that means for your message, tone, visual direction, content and website journey.

Brand decision

The final wording, page structure, colour direction, content theme or call to action that follows from the strategy.

03

Six questions before anything else.

These are the questions that stop your website, visual identity and social media becoming vague, inconsistent or difficult to recognise yourself in.

01

Who are you?

Your story, values, working style, philosophy and professional identity.

02

Who do you help?

The people your communication is speaking to and the state they may be in when they arrive.

03

What change do you help create?

The emotional and practical transformation your work supports.

04

Why would someone choose you?

Your strengths, perspective, fit, boundaries and genuine positioning.

05

How should people experience you?

The tone, atmosphere, trust signals and emotional quality of the brand.

06

What should someone do next?

The website journey, calls to action, enquiry experience, content priorities and future marketing decisions.

Questions people often ask.

Do I need an existing website?

No. Some people come with an existing website that no longer reflects their work. Others are starting completely from scratch.

Can I start with the Brand Clarity Blueprint only?

Yes. The Blueprint can stand alone. You can use it to clarify your direction, improve your current website, plan your content or decide whether a full redesign is the right next step.

Do I have to buy every stage?

No. Each stage is optional. You can start with Brand Clarity and stop there, or add Visual Identity, Website design or Hosting & Care when it makes sense.

What happens in the Brand Clarity Session?

We talk through your story, values, clients, working style, positioning and communication needs. The session is designed to draw out the material your Blueprint, visual identity, website or future content can be built from.

How is the Blueprint evidence-based?

The Blueprint is based on what you say, repeat, emphasise and demonstrate during the Brand Clarity process. The aim is not to invent a brand for you, but to identify what is already there and translate it into clear communication.

Will I see the reasoning behind recommendations?

Yes. The process is designed so recommendations can be traced back to evidence, interpretation and practical implications for your brand, website and messaging.

Do you use AI during the process?

I may use carefully supervised tools to help organise notes, transcripts and early draft material. The judgement, interpretation, recommendations and final direction are reviewed and shaped by me. AI does not replace the listening, thinking or decision-making.

What if I don't know what I want to say?

That is one of the most common reasons people get in touch. You do not need to arrive with perfect wording. The process is designed to help us find it.

Can you help with the wording?

Yes. Messaging is central to the work. We shape the language before and during the design process, so the website is not just attractive but clear.

How long does a project usually take?

It depends which stage you choose. A Brand Clarity Blueprint is quicker than a full website. Complete website projects usually take around four to six weeks from first conversation to launch, depending on the size of the site and how quickly feedback is provided.

Do you provide hosting?

Yes. I offer an optional Hosting & Care plan for hosting, maintenance, security updates and support after launch.

Who do you work with?

I mainly work with therapists, counsellors, coaches and helping professionals whose work depends on trust, relationship and careful communication.
