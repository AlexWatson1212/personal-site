About

Branding, websites and strategy for people whose work depends on trust.

My background is in marketing, branding and communication. I have a degree in Marketing Psychology and over ten years of experience helping businesses think about how they are seen, understood and trusted.

I have worked with businesses across a wide range of industries, from engineering and beauty to hospitality, retail and local service businesses.

I originally learned website design as a practical way to bring branding, positioning and communication together. The website became the place where strategy could be seen, felt and experienced.

I am also training as a counsellor, which has deepened my interest in trust, human behaviour and meaningful communication. That combination shapes the way I work: calm design, clear messaging and websites that feel honest, grounded and human.

My aim is to help therapists, counsellors, coaches and other helping professionals create thoughtful, professional websites without agency-level fees or the stress of figuring everything out alone.

Clear message. Calm design. Human trust. A website should not just look professional. It should help people understand who you are and feel confident taking the next step.

FAQ

Practical questions.

A few simple answers before getting in touch.

Will I own my website?

Yes. The website is created for you. If you decide to move away in the future, the files can be supplied so you are not trapped.

Can I use my own domain?

Yes. I can help connect your existing domain or guide you through buying one if you do not already have one.

Do I need to know exactly what I want?

No. Part of the work is helping you clarify your message, structure and direction before the website is built.

Can you help with the words?

Yes. I can help shape the wording so your website sounds clear, professional and human without feeling overly salesy.

Is this only for therapists?

No. I mainly focus on therapists, counsellors, coaches and helping professionals, but the same approach can work for other meaningful, trust-based services.

What if I already have a website?

I can review your current site and suggest what could be made clearer, calmer or more effective before deciding whether a rebuild is needed.
