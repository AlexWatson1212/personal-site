04

Premium does not mean louder.

For therapists and helping professionals, a premium website is not about pressure, polish for its own sake or making everything sound bigger than it is.

It is about careful wording, generous space, warm imagery, thoughtful structure and a simple path to enquiry.

The website should feel like the beginning of a safe conversation — not a sales page, not a template, and not a performance.

I help therapists communicate what makes their work different.

My background is in marketing, branding and communication. Alongside this, I am training as a counsellor, giving me a deeper understanding of the relationship therapists hope to build before a client ever makes contact.

That perspective shapes every project. Rather than beginning with colours or layouts, we begin by understanding your practice, your values and the people you want to help.

The result is a website that reflects who you are, explains your work clearly and helps the right people feel confident about taking the next step.

Good design isn't about saying more. It's about helping people understand what matters.

09

Questions people often ask.

Do I need an existing website?

No. Some people come with an existing website, while others are starting completely from scratch.

Can I start with the Brand Clarity Blueprint only?

Yes. Many people begin with the Blueprint before deciding whether they would like me to build the website as well.

What if I don't know what I want to say?

That's one of the most common reasons people get in touch. The Blueprint is designed to help you find the words before any design work begins.

Can you help with the wording?

Yes. Messaging is a central part of the process, not an afterthought.

How long does a project usually take?

Most projects take around four to six weeks from our first conversation to launch, depending on the size of the website and how quickly content and feedback are provided.

Do you provide hosting?

Yes. I offer an optional Hosting & Care plan, including hosting, maintenance, security updates and support after launch.

Who do you work with?

I mainly work with therapists, counsellors, coaches and other helping professionals whose work depends on trust, relationships and clear communication.
