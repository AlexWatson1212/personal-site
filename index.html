Why I do this

I understand the challenge of building trust online.

As a trainee counsellor, I know how hard it can be to describe personal, sensitive or emotionally meaningful work online without sounding corporate, clinical or overly salesy.

People often arrive at a therapist or service-based website feeling vulnerable, unsure and overwhelmed. A good website should help them feel calmer, clearer and more confident about reaching out.

My aim is simple: to create websites that feel human, trustworthy and easy to navigate, while still looking polished and professional.
