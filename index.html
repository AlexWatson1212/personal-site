About

A quieter, more personal way of building therapist websites.

I’m Alex — a therapist in training, father, and designer. I care about how a website feels, not just how it functions.

Most therapist websites are either too clinical, too generic, or too busy. My aim is to create something simpler: a site that feels calm to land on, clear to move through, and human enough to trust.
