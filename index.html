About

A more personal way of building therapist websites.

I’m Alex — a therapist in training, father, and designer with a strong interest in how people experience safety, trust, and emotional clarity online.

Most therapist websites are either too clinical, too generic, or too techy. They may contain all the right information, but they do not always feel the way therapy itself needs to feel.

My approach is simple: create websites that are calm to look at, easy to navigate, and written in a way that helps the right person feel more at ease.
