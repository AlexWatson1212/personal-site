The real problem

A website is not just a design job.

Most people do not struggle because they need more pages, more effects or a more complicated website. They struggle because they are not yet clear on what their website needs to communicate.

Before the website comes a simpler question: what does someone need to understand, feel and trust before they are ready to get in touch?

About

Understanding people first. Building websites second.

My background is in marketing, branding and communication. I have a degree in Marketing Psychology and over ten years of experience helping businesses think about how they are seen, understood and trusted.

I originally learned website design as a practical way to bring branding, positioning and communication together. The website became the place where the thinking could be seen, felt and used.

I am also training as a counsellor, which has deepened my interest in trust, human behaviour and meaningful communication. That combination shapes the way I work: calm design, clear messaging and websites that feel honest, grounded and human.

The aim is simple: a website that helps people quickly understand who you are, how you work and whether you might be the right person to contact.
