Practice Clarity

One final question.

Practice Clarity began with one question.

What is your website reflecting?

It ends with another.

How might someone feel after spending time with your practice?

Those two questions may appear unrelated. In reality, they are asking exactly the same thing.

As your practice evolves, your website will evolve too. Not because it needs to become more persuasive. Because it should continue becoming a more faithful reflection of the therapist you are becoming.

If you return to these principles over the coming years, I hope you don't find yourself trying to remember every recommendation.

I hope you simply return to one quiet habit.

Whenever something feels uncertain... ask yourself: What unnecessary uncertainty could I remove?

That question has quietly guided every page in this framework. It will probably continue guiding your practice long after you've forgotten where you first read it.

Thank you for reading.

— Alexander Watson
